About Us

Waste Management Association(WMA) is one of Rocklin High School's largest community service clubs! We are dedicated to keeping the school clean, and do some after school projects as well! You can join today, and make the world a better place!

Our Future Projects

Spring View

Larger park… more trash! This will most likely be a competitive Trash Clash, with teams splitting up all over the park. More info to follow!

Miner's Ravine

Still a hypothetical - but we are looking into walking the short Miner’s Ravine Trail in Roseville and picking up any trash we find along the way. Stay tuned!

Trashy Dollar

One of our more ambitious projects - we will are looking into setting up trash cans and/or recycling bins in the dollar tree are to reduce waste!
